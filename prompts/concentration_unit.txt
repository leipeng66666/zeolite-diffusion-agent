## Target unit: mol/L (moles per liter)

## For each input unit, classify into one of three operations:

### Operation "multiply" — unit needs conversion to mol/L
Output: <unit> → multiply | <factor>
- <factor> is the numeric multiplier: converted_value = original_value × factor
- Output factor as a plain decimal (e.g., 0.001 or 1e-3, both are fine)
- Examples:
  mmol/L → multiply | 0.001           (1 mmol = 10⁻³ mol)
  mmol/ml → multiply | 1.0            (mmol/mL = mol/L, same)
  μmol/L → multiply | 1e-6            (1 μmol = 10⁻⁶ mol)
  mol/ml → multiply | 1000            (mol/mL = 1000 mol/L)
  mol/m³ → multiply | 0.001           (1 mol/m³ = 0.001 mol/L)
  mmol/g_zeolite → multiply | ...     (depends on zeolite density, unless stated)
- For units with EMBEDDED scale factors like "10^{-3} mol/L":
  This means the value is in 10⁻³ mol/L units. To get plain mol/L: multiply by 10⁻³.
  10^{-3} mol/L → multiply | 0.001

### Operation "identity" — already in target unit mol/L
Output: <unit> → identity | 1.0
- For units that are mol/L regardless of notation:
  mol/L → identity | 1.0
  mol l⁻¹ → identity | 1.0
  mol·L⁻¹ → identity | 1.0
  mole/L → identity | 1.0
  M → identity | 1.0               (Molar = mol/L)
- The value does not change, only the unit notation may be normalized.

### Operation "keep" — NOT converted, keep as-is
Output: <unit> → keep | N/A
- For units that cannot be converted to mol/L without additional information:
  mg/L → keep | N/A                (need molecular weight)
  g/L → keep | N/A                 (need molecular weight)
  wt% → keep | N/A                 (mass fraction, need density/MW)
  vol% → keep | N/A                (volume fraction)

  wt ppm → keep | N/A
  molecules/unit cell → keep | N/A (zeolite-specific, not fluid concentration)
  molecules/nm³ → keep | N/A       (number density, need T/P)
- For null/empty/none/NaN units:
  none → keep | N/A
